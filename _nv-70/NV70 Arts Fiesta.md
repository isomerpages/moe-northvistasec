<h4><strong>NV70 Arts Fiesta (14 November 2026)</strong></h4>
<p>The NV70 Arts Fiesta is a celebratory showcase of our performing arts
CCAs, bringing together students, staff, parents and alumni for an afternoon
of music, dance and drama. It will also be the platform to launch the NV70
Stories book, with readings and a small gallery of featured pages. The
programme blends performance with community moments so that relationships
are renewed, pride is deepened and our 德新 story continues to grow.</p>
