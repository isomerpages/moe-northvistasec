<h4><strong>NV70 Tree Planting Initiative (30 – 31 March 2026)</strong></h4>
<p>The NV70 Tree Planting Initiative at Pulau Ubin is a signature anniversary
activity that brings our 德新 family together in purposeful action. Across
two sessions, we will plant 70 trees, one for each year of North Vista’s
journey, guided by NParks and supported by our staff and student leaders.
The first session will involve current students, building stewardship and
teamwork in the field. The second session will bring together representatives
from students, staff, parents, alumni, and partners for a simple ceremony
and collective planting to mark our shared milestone.</p>
