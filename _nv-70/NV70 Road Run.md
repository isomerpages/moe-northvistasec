<h4><strong>NV70 Road Run (22 May 2026)</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/NV70/VRR/VRR_1.png">
</div>
<p>We brought the NV70 edition of our Vistarian Road Run back to our homeground,
where the race was run along Punggol Park, and the post-run festivities
were held back in school. The annual event that sparks joy in our school
community saw students taking on a 2km race route as they competed to be
the fastest boy and girl in their cohort. After the run, students then
had the opportunity to learn sporting lessons from their teachers through
teacher-student games like Captain’s Ball, Basketball and Badminton. There
were also challenge stations that put their sporting skills to the test,
as they sought to become the highest scorers in the various 70-seconds
challenges. The morning culminated in the whole school population coming
together to form the NV70 formation in our parade square. As always, staff
and students had an enjoyable morning sweating it out under the sun and
experiencing the highs and lows of competition. Most importantly, everyone
came together in great spirits, and as one Vistarian family, to celebrate
a school event together.</p>
