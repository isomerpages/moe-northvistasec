<h4><strong>NV70 Speech &amp; Prize Giving Day (24 April 2026)</strong></h4>
<p>The NV70 Speech and Prize Giving Day is our formal celebration of achievement
and service. As part of NV70 celebrations, this momentous Speech Day marks
seven decades of educational excellence and community spirit. Families
and guests gather for a dignified ceremony featuring student performances
and stories that reflect the spirit of 德新 (NV), as we honour our shared
journey and renew pride in our identity.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Congratulations to our award recipients for their outstanding achievement,
dedication, and exemplary conduct. Your hard work and perseverance continue
to inspire the school community.</p>
