<h4><strong>NV70 Homecoming &amp; Open House (14 November 2026)</strong></h4>

students and families while reconnecting with alumni, staff and current
students. The day blends discovery of NV programmes with moments that honour
our shared journey, so that relationships are renewed and pride in our
identity grows.</p>
