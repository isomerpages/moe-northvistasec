<h3>Special Education Needs</h3>
<p><strong>MOE’s vision</strong>&nbsp;is for&nbsp;all students&nbsp;in every
school to&nbsp;experience an inclusive culture, one where</p>
<p>1 . Every Child can learn, and children with needs receive timely and
appropriate support to achieve their full potential</p>
<p>2 . Every teacher is skillful to support and mange diverse learners, including
students with SEN</p>
<p>3 . Every school has its own systematic whole-school approach to support
diverse learners, including students with SEN</p>
<p>The school and the home are two of the most important systems for our
students.&nbsp;When in sync, it brings about many positive students' outcomes
in terms of academic, behavioural and emotional well-being.&nbsp;We are
privileged to walk this journey with you as we work as a team to develop
the student.</p>
<p>We have included resources which aim to provide parent/staff with information
and self-help tools, to better support students with SEN.</p>
