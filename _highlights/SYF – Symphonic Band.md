<h4><strong>SYF 2025 Arts Presentation for Concert Band</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/NV Highlights/Band/Band_SYF_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Our Band members participated in the SYF Arts Presentation on Thursday,
3 April, at Singapore Conference Hall. Our boys and girls performed two
pieces:</p>
<p>• SET PIECE: OVERTURE NO. 1 (COMPOSER: KELLY TANG)</p>
<p>• CHOICE PIECE: KING’S MOUNTAIN ADVENTURE (COMPOSER: ED HUCKEBY)</p>
<p>We are proud to announce that our Symphonic Band achieved a Certificate
of Accomplishment.</p>
<p>The band also participated in the SYF Arts Presentation for Percussion
Ensemble. A group of 9 students were involved in the presentation, which
took place on Tuesday, 29 April, at Singapore Conference Hall as well.
They performed two pieces:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>TITLE: WADATSUMI (海神) [COMPOSER: DAIYA HAMAGUCHI]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>TITLE: BATTERY [COMPOSER: ANG MING WEI MARK EMMANUEL JR]</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We are very pleased to announce our group of 9 achieved a Certificate
of Accomplishment too.</p>
