<h4><strong>SYF 2025 Arts Presentation for Drama</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/NV Highlights/Drama/Drama_SYF_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Our Drama Club took part in the SYF Arts Presentation on Wednesday, 9
April, at the Gateway Theatre, performing a piece titled “Caged”.</p>
<p>While the cast brought the story to life on stage, a hardworking team
behind the scenes handled everything from stage management and lighting
to sound, surtitles, props, and costumes.</p>
<p>We are very proud to announce that the team was awarded a <strong>Certificate of Distinction</strong> for
their outstanding efforts and performance!</p>

<h4><strong>SYF 2023 Arts Presentation for Drama</strong></h4>
<p>Our Drama Club members participated in the SYF Arts Presentation on Wednesday,
12 April, at the Gateway Theatre. The title of our performance is “Lighthouse”.</p>
<p>We are proud to announce that our boys and girls achieved a <strong>Certificate of Accomplishment.</strong>
